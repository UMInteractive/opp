About Open Privacy Policy

Open Privacy Policy helps you create a clear privacy policy for customers to understand what happens to their personal data.

Clear privacy policies build better brands

Let's face it — it also helps us build a better Internet.

We know web sites collect user data for evil purposes, but many collect data to provide better services and experiences or improve the design of their product. We just ask they do a better job of letting us know how they use our data.

Open Privacy Policy will help most companies generate a clear, easy-to-use privacy policy which explains how you collect, store and use data, whether it is shared with third parties and what rights a user has over their information.

Creating a clear privacy policy will help your organization establish a relationship of trust with between users and your brand.

What is Open Privacy Policy?

Open Privacy Policy is a free service that believes everyone is better off when companies are clear about how they use personal data. Customers are better served by clear policies, and companies benefit by building trust.

Open Privacy Policy can help generate a clear, easy-to-use privacy policy that quickly explains how you collect, share, and secure customer data.

How does it work?

All you have to do to get your free privacy policy is answer a few questions about your privacy practices so we can generate your policy. When you generate your privacy policy with us, you are allowed to add our badge to the footer of your site and use our icons to illustrate your policy. These simple, clear visual cues assure users that your brand is transparent and trustworthy. That way, when consumers click I agree, they really mean it.

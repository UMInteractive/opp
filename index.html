What is Open Privacy Policy?

Open Privacy Policy is a free service for generating a clear, easy-to-use privacy policy which explains how you collect, share and secure customer data.

Everyone is better off knowing how personal data is used. Customers are better served by clear policies and develop trust in companies.

How does it work?

When you generate your privacy policy with us, you are allowed to add our badge to the footer of your site and use our icons to illustrate your policy. These simple, visual cues assure users your brand is transparent and trustworthy. That way, when consumers click "I Agree," they really mean it.
